Stupa Ulun Danu - Bali

Stupa Ulun Danu is a part of the iconic Ulun Danu Beratan Temple complex, situated on the serene shores of Lake Beratan in Bali. This beautiful temple is dedicated to Dewi Danu, the goddess of lakes and rivers, and is one of the most picturesque and tranquil spots in Indonesia.

Built in the 17th century, Ulun Danu Beratan features stunning traditional Balinese architecture, blending seamlessly with the natural surroundings. The temple is a harmonious mix of Hindu and Buddhist elements, symbolizing the unity of Indonesia's spiritual heritage.

The stupa at Ulun Danu is a significant feature, representing Buddhist influence in the predominantly Hindu temple complex. It is surrounded by meticulously maintained gardens and offers breathtaking views of the lake and surrounding mountains.
